Core Features

Built for the work,
not the show.

[ 01 ]

Item Tracking

Track every product by SKU, category, location, and unit. Add custom fields to match how your operation actually runs.

[ 02 ]

Stock Movements

Log incoming stock, outgoing orders, and adjustments with full audit trail. Every move is dated and attributed.

[ 03 ]

Threshold Alerts

Set minimum stock levels per item. Get notified before you run out — not after a customer already noticed.

[ 04 ]

Supplier Records

Maintain a clean supplier directory. Link items to suppliers so reordering is a lookup, not a memory test.

[ 05 ]

Reports & Exports

Pull reports on stock value, movement history, and variance. Export to CSV or PDF in a single click.

[ 06 ]

Multi-user Access

Invite team members with role-based permissions. Managers see everything. Staff see what they need.
